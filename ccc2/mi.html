Membership Information

Whether exploring the reservoir or nearby waterways of Weaselhead in a recreational canoe or kayak, or looking for a new outdoor alternative to staying in (or getting into) shape. Make the sport of paddling a pursuit in your life with a low-cost membership to the Calgary Canoe Club.


How to Apply:


  • Download a Membership Form (PDF) and Waiver Form (PDF) and mail or e-mail or bring in person to the Calgary Canoe Club. You can pay by Cheque, MC, Visa, Debit, or Cash. Email:
    calgarycanoeclubinfo@gmail.com
  • Visit us as of May 1st, boat bays will be open, weather permitting

If you have any questions please head to our FAQ's page as it may be answered there.

Note: To open the registration form and waiver, you will need to use Adobe Reader. To download your free copy, visit www.adobe.com

Membership Rates


Effective May 1, 2014 to October 31, 2014 (Weather permitting)

Junior (17 and under): $60
Student: $100
Single: $140
Family: $225
Prices include GST


Membership is open to the public and provides the following:

  • Free on-reservoir use of the Calgary Canoe Club's recreational canoes and kayaks. Due to liability restrictions we do not allow for off-site rentals.
  • Use of PFD's and paddles for canoe and kayaks
  • Access to Marathon, Outrigger, Sprint Racing competitive programs.

Boat Availability

We have 30 whitewater kayaks and 28 two man recreation canoes. Boats are available on a first come first serve basis, it is rare we run out. The boat bay staff can let you know if we have any large kayak groups booked (this occurs on a few occasions in July and August 2-5:30pm).


Prerequisites Required?

No prerequisites are required for membership. We strongly suggest you take a lesson on canoeing and kayaking before going on the water. Boat bay staff will offer a ‘dryland’ orientation for all new members to the equipment during business hours. Schedule a lesson by either calling or emailing our office during business hours Monday- Friday.
Cost: $40/lesson.

Guest Policies

Members are encouraged to bring guests to the club and introduce them to paddling. Guests are allowed to join members in a canoe at any time. However, if you would like to introduce kayaking please ensure you go over the basics with them. If a staff member is available we would be happy to give them a brief dry land orientation.

Your guest will not be charged if they are sharing a canoe with you. Guest fees are charged for additional boats (canoes and kayaks).
Guest fees: Full day, $20. Half day, $10. This includes a maximum of 2 guests.

Please note: Canoes may be rented only when the guest is accompanied by a member.

Disclosure of Personal Information

The Calgary Canoe Club will not trade, rent or sell your personal information without your prior consent.
